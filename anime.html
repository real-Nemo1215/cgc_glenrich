What is anime?

Anime is a style of animation from Japan known for its colorful art, unique character designs, and wide range of genres. It includes TV shows, movies, and original videos, often adapted from Japanese comics called manga. Anime features diverse stories, from action and romance to fantasy and sci-fi, appealing to various age groups and tastes. Popular examples include "Naruto," "One Piece," and Studio Ghibli films like "Spirited Away." Its distinctive style and storytelling have gained a global fanbase.
